## Sources to Avoid as Main Evidence

These can help discover leads but should not carry final thesis claims if official or academic sources are available:

| Source | Reason |
|---|---|
| The Guardian | News source; replace trade remedy facts with MOFCOM / WTO / EU legal acts |
| China Briefing | Business commentary; replace with EU or Chinese official source |
| White & Case | Law firm commentary; replace with Commission FSR documents or legal acts |
| Ashurst | Law firm commentary; replace with Commission trade defence annual report |
| Chambers | Legal commentary; replace with EUR-Lex / Commission IPI documents |
| European Sources Online | Aggregator; follow through to original EU document |
| Global Times | State-aligned media; use only as media framing, not evidence |
| Wikipedia | Do not use |
